# Collected Medical Prompts (Raw Data)

## Diagnosis & Clinical Assessment
1. **Medical History Analysis**: "As an experienced medical professional, examine and summarize the patient's medical history. Highlight any recurrent health issues, chronic diseases, or potential risk factors based on the given health records."
2. **Physical Examination Guide**: "As a medical professional, guide me through the key steps of a general physical examination. Remember to include vital signs check, general appearance, and the examination of the main body systems."
3. **Differential Diagnosis**: "Act as a knowledgeable physician assistant to diagnose the potential diseases from the following symptoms and history: [Patient Data]. Analyze these symptoms and provide a potential diagnosis."
4. **Symptom Analysis**: "Analyze the following symptoms [Symptoms] in a [Age/Gender] patient with a history of [History]. Provide a list of potential causes ranked by likelihood."

## Treatment & Management
5. **Medication Suggestion**: "Act as a medical assistant to suggest possible medications for the following symptoms and patient history: [Patient Data]. Include standard dosages and contraindications."
6. **Treatment Planning**: "Act as a general practitioner and design a preliminary treatment plan based on the following patient information: [Symptoms, History, Vitals]. Please suggest a potential treatment plan including lifestyle changes and medical interventions."
7. **Chronic Disease Management**: "Write a step-by-step guide for patients on managing [Chronic Condition] through lifestyle modifications, medication adherence, and monitoring."

## Patient Communication
8. **Addressing Concerns**: "As a knowledgeable medical assistant, address the concerns and fears of a patient who has just been diagnosed with [Condition]. The patient is worried about [Specific Concerns]. Provide reassurance and factual information."
9. **Health Advice**: "As an AI knowledgeable about general health and wellness, provide tips on maintaining a balanced diet and regular exercise routine for a patient with [Condition]."
10. **Explaining Procedures**: "Explain the [Medical Procedure] to a patient with low health literacy. Use simple analogies and avoid jargon."
11. **Bad News Delivery**: "Help me explain [Difficult Diagnosis] to a patient. What questions might they ask? How do I address fear? Structure the conversation using the SPIKES protocol."

## Administrative & Documentation
12. **Lab Result Interpretation**: "As a medical expert, interpret these laboratory results and provide a brief analysis. Here are the patient's lab results: [Lab Data]. Highlight any values outside the normal range and suggest clinical correlations."
13. **Follow-up Consultation**: "Act as a professional doctor conducting a follow-up consultation with a patient. Based on their medical history of [History] and current status, list the key questions to ask and signs to look for."
14. **Referral Letter**: "Draft a referral letter to a [Specialist] for a patient with [Condition]. Include relevant history, current medications, and the reason for referral."

## Research & Writing
15. **Literature Summary**: "Summarise recent studies on the effectiveness of [Intervention] in reducing [Outcome]."
16. **Topic Overview**: "Discuss the latest advancements in [Medical Field] and its potential applications in treating [Condition]."
17. **Patient Education Material**: "Develop a patient-friendly brochure explaining the importance of [Preventive Measure] and addressing common concerns."
18. **Paper Section Drafting**: "Draft the Introduction section for a case report on [Rare Condition], focusing on its prevalence and diagnostic challenges."

## Education & Learning
19. **Concept Explanation**: "Explain the pathophysiology of [Disease] as if teaching a first-year medical student."
20. **Quiz Generation**: "Create 5 multiple-choice questions to test knowledge on the management of [Acute Condition], including explanations for the correct answers."

## Radiology & Imaging
21. **X-Ray Interpretation Assistant**: "Act as a radiologist assistant. I will provide a description of an X-ray image. Help me generate a structured report including findings, impression, and recommendations. [Image Description]"
22. **MRI Report Simplification**: "Rewrite this MRI report in simple language for a patient to understand. Explain technical terms like 'hyperintensity' and 'stenosis'."
23. **Imaging Protocol Suggestion**: "Suggest the appropriate imaging protocol for a patient presenting with [Symptoms] suspected of [Condition], considering radiation exposure and diagnostic yield."

## Nursing & Care Planning
24. **Nursing Care Plan Generator**: "Create a comprehensive nursing care plan for a patient with [Condition]. Include nursing diagnoses (NANDA-I), outcomes (NOC), and interventions (NIC)."
25. **Discharge Education Plan**: "Develop a discharge teaching plan for an elderly patient with [Condition], focusing on medication safety, fall prevention, and dietary restrictions."
26. **Shift Handoff Report**: "Draft a structured SBAR (Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation) handoff report for a patient who [Patient Status/Events]."

## Medical Education (Anatomy & Physiology)
27. **Anatomy Tutor**: "Act as a knowledgeable anatomy tutor. Explain the [Anatomical Structure], its relations, blood supply, and innervation. Use mnemonics to help me remember."
28. **Physiology Mechanism Explanation**: "Explain the physiological mechanism of [Process, e.g., Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System] step-by-step, highlighting key enzymes and regulation points."
29. **Case-Based Learning Scenario**: "Generate a clinical case scenario involving a patient with [Condition] to test my understanding of [Physiological Concept]. Include follow-up questions."

## Mental Health & Psychiatry
30. **Mental Status Exam Documentation**: "Help me structure the documentation of a Mental Status Examination (MSE) based on these notes: [Clinical Notes]. Ensure professional terminology."
31. **CBT Homework Assignment**: "Suggest 3 Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) homework assignments for a patient struggling with [Specific Issue, e.g., Social Anxiety]."
32. **Psychoeducation Script**: "Write a script to explain the concept of [Psychological Concept, e.g., Neuroplasticity] to a patient with depression to encourage engagement in therapy."

## Research & Academic Writing (Advanced)
33. **Case Report Abstract**: "Draft an abstract for a medical case report about [Rare Condition]. Structure it with Introduction, Case Presentation, and Conclusion."
34. **Literature Review Outline**: "Create a detailed outline for a literature review on [Topic]. Include key themes, potential subheadings, and types of studies to look for."
35. **Statistical Method Explanation**: "Explain the difference between [Statistical Test A] and [Statistical Test B] and when to use each in clinical research."
